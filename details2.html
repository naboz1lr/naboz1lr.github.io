Context:Web Design 1 had us brainstorm, research, rationalize and prototype an idea for an app based off the prompt of productivity. All while working with a peer.

The Challenge:Find a gimmick that was backed by research and was reasonably original. Researching competitors and creating personas and user journeys.

The Solution: We settled on an app that leveraged the benefits of gamification. The app acted as a planner with a focus on collecting bonsai trees that used AI to automatically create tasks and due dates based on a one sentence input.
