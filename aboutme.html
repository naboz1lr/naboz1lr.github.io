Hello!

Hi, I’m Logan, a junior studying Graphic Design at Central Michigan University. I’m really into typography and web design, and I love making visuals that are both clean and easy to use. Whether I’m building a new brand identity or trying out different design techniques, my goal is always to solve problems creatively while keeping the details sharp.
