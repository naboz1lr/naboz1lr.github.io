Rooted
December 12 2025

Context: For a typography assignment, I developed an original typeface using FontStruct and was tasked with designing a promotional poster to showcase the font’s characters and practical applications.

The Challenge:Seamlessly integrating all required informational text into a unique, cohesive visual composition without overcrowding the design or detracting from the typeface itself.

The Solution: I created a vector illustration of a vintage CRT television to serve as the focal point, naturally embedding the typography into the screen and casing. This project marked a significant turning point in my design process, sparking a deep commitment to meticulous refinement and pixel-perfect detailing.
