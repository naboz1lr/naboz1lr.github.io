December 12 2025

Context: For an Advanced Typography project, I was tasked with formatting a multi-host podcast transcript into a cohesive, printed booklet.

The Challenge:To establish a clear typographic hierarchy that managed the continuous back-and-forth dialogue of three main hosts, while developing a unique visual style that added character without sacrificing readability.

The Solution: I designed a layout inspired by medieval manuscripts using typefaces from the P22 type foundry. To ensure legibility and easy comprehension, I developed a strict text hierarchy: bold typography for speaker names paired with indented, lighter gray body text to seamlessly guide the reader through the conversation.
