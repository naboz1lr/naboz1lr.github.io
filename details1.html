Documents on Demand
February 15 2026

Context: Documents on Demand is a library service providing material scans and interlibrary loans. As a student assistant, I was tasked with redesigning the department's brand identity.

The Challenge: Adhering strictly to the university's maroon and gold style guide, and avoiding overused visual clichés typical of "on-demand" services.

The Solution: After iterating and scrapping early concepts to ensure total originality, I developed a modern, clean visual system that effectively communicates the service's purpose while staying fully brand-compliant and representing my style.
